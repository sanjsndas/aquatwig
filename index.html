Expert Tips for Small-Space Irrigation

Sunlight Considerations

Plants in full sun need more frequent watering than those in shade. Position emitters closer together for sun-loving plants and adjust your controller accordingly.

Wind Exposure

Balconies on higher floors often experience more wind, which can dry out plants faster. Consider adding windbreaks or increasing watering duration for exposed locations.

Plant Grouping

Group plants with similar water needs together. This allows you to zone your irrigation system efficiently, giving each group exactly what they need without overwatering others.
