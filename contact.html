Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products, services, and support options below. If you don't find what you're looking for, please don't hesitate to contact us directly.

1. How do I schedule a consultation for my small-space irrigation system?

You can schedule a consultation by filling out our contact form, calling our office at (212) 555-1234, or emailing consultations@aquatwig.com. Our team typically responds within 24-48 hours to schedule an appointment. Consultations can be conducted in-person at your location, virtually via video call, or at our showroom in New York. We'll discuss your space dimensions, plant types, water source, and budget to recommend the best irrigation solution.

2. What information should I have ready when contacting support?

When contacting our support team, please have your product model number (found on the product label), purchase date, and a detailed description of the issue ready. Photos or videos of the problem can be extremely helpful. If your system has a controller, note any error messages displayed. For installation questions, have your space dimensions and photos of the area available. This information helps us provide faster, more accurate assistance.

3. What are your customer service hours?

Our customer service team is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM EST, and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM EST. For urgent matters outside these hours, you can leave a voicemail or send an email, and we'll respond as soon as possible when we reopen. During peak seasons (spring and early summer), we extend our hours to accommodate higher demand. Emergency support for commercial clients is available 24/7 through our premium support plan.

4. How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive an email with tracking information. You can also log in to your account on our website to view order status and tracking details. For larger systems that ship in multiple packages, each component will have its own tracking number. If you haven't received tracking information within 3 business days of placing your order, please contact our sales team at sales@aquatwig.com or call (212) 555-5678 during business hours.

5. What's the best way to get technical support for my system?

For technical support, we recommend starting with our online troubleshooting guides and video tutorials available in the Support section of our website. If you still need assistance, you can contact our technical support team via phone at (212) 555-9012, email at support@aquatwig.com, or through live chat on our website during business hours. For complex issues, remote diagnostic sessions are available where our technicians can guide you through solutions in real-time.

6. Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we offer professional installation services in the New York metropolitan area and select other regions. Our certified technicians can install your entire system, provide partial installation assistance, or offer guidance for DIY installation. Installation services can be requested during the consultation or purchase process. For areas outside our direct service coverage, we maintain a network of certified installers we can refer you to. Installation pricing varies based on system complexity and location.

7. How do I return or exchange a product?

To initiate a return or exchange, please contact our customer service team within 30 days of purchase. You'll need your order number and reason for return. Most unused products in original packaging can be returned for a full refund, minus shipping costs. Exchanges can be processed for products of equal or greater value. Some large or custom systems may have different return policies - please check your order details or contact us for specifics. Return shipping labels are provided for defective items.

8. Where can I purchase AquaTwig products?

AquaTwig products are available through our website, our New York showroom, and select authorized dealers nationwide. You can use our dealer locator tool on our website to find retailers near you. We also participate in several home and garden shows throughout the year where you can see our products in person and speak with representatives. For commercial or bulk purchases, please contact our sales team directly for special pricing and options.

9. How do I become an authorized dealer?

We're always interested in partnering with quality retailers who share our commitment to sustainable irrigation solutions. Prospective dealers can apply through the "Become a Dealer" section of our website or contact our wholesale department at wholesale@aquatwig.com. Requirements include a physical retail location (or established online presence), relevant industry experience, and the ability to provide proper product training to customers. We offer marketing support, display materials, and training to all authorized dealers.

10. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for online orders. In our showroom, we also accept cash and checks with proper identification. For commercial accounts, we offer net-30 terms with approved credit. Financing options are available through third-party providers for qualified customers. All transactions are processed securely, and we never store your full payment information on our servers.

11. How long does shipping typically take?

Standard shipping within the continental U.S. takes 3-5 business days after processing. Processing time is typically 1-2 business days for in-stock items. Expedited shipping options are available at checkout. For custom or made-to-order systems, lead times vary from 1-3 weeks depending on complexity - our sales team will provide an estimated timeline when you place your order. International shipping is available to select countries with longer transit times. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

12. Do you offer volume discounts for large orders?

Yes, we offer quantity discounts for large residential and commercial orders. Discount tiers start at 10+ units of the same product. For custom projects or very large orders, please contact our sales team at sales@aquatwig.com or call (212) 555-5678 to discuss pricing. We can also create customized quotes for unique projects that bundle products and services. Contractors, landscape designers, and property managers may qualify for our professional discount program with proof of business.

13. What's your warranty policy?

Most AquaTwig products come with a 3-year limited warranty against defects in materials and workmanship. Smart controllers have a 2-year warranty. Warranty coverage begins from the date of purchase and requires proof of purchase for claims. The warranty doesn't cover damage from improper installation, accidents, or normal wear and tear. Some components like tubing and emitters have prorated warranties. Complete warranty details are included with each product and available on our website. Our support team can help with warranty claims.

14. How do I register my product for warranty?

Product registration is quick and easy through our website's warranty registration page. You'll need your product serial number (found on the product or packaging) and proof of purchase. Registration takes about 2 minutes and ensures we can contact you if there are any product updates or recalls. While registration isn't required for warranty coverage, it does make the claims process faster if needed. You can also register by mail using the warranty card included with your product.

15. Do you offer maintenance services?

Yes, we offer seasonal maintenance packages for residential and commercial irrigation systems in our service areas. Services include system startup in spring, winterization in fall, and periodic check-ups during the growing season. Our technicians can also perform one-time maintenance or repairs as needed. Maintenance plans can be customized based on your system type and plant needs. Contact our service department at service@aquatwig.com or call (212) 555-7890 for pricing and availability.

16. Can I visit your showroom?

Our New York showroom at 100 Bleecker St is open to the public Monday through Friday from 10:00 AM to 6:00 PM and Saturdays from 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. No appointment is necessary for general browsing, but we recommend scheduling an appointment if you'd like a personalized consultation or demonstration of specific products. Our showroom features working displays of all our irrigation systems, product samples you can examine, and knowledgeable staff to answer your questions.

17. Do you offer educational resources?

Absolutely! We offer free workshops at our showroom monthly covering topics like small-space irrigation design, water conservation techniques, and system maintenance. We also have an extensive online resource library with how-to guides, videos, and blog articles. For professionals, we offer accredited CEU courses for landscape architects and contractors. Schools and community groups can request educational presentations. Visit the "Learn" section of our website or contact education@aquatwig.com for more information.

18. How do I request a quote for a custom system?

For custom irrigation system quotes, please use the "Request a Quote" form on our website or email quotes@aquatwig.com with details about your project. Include measurements or diagrams of your space, photos, plant types, and any specific requirements. Our design team will review your information and typically respond with a preliminary quote within 3 business days. For complex projects, we may schedule a consultation (virtual or in-person) to discuss options before providing a final quote.

19. What if I need parts for an older system?

We maintain an extensive inventory of replacement parts for current and discontinued AquaTwig systems. Contact our parts department at parts@aquatwig.com or call (212) 555-3456 with your system model number (usually found on the controller or main valve) and a description of the needed part. For systems more than 10 years old, some components may no longer be available, but we can often suggest compatible modern replacements. Photos of the part or where it fits in your system can help us identify what you need.

20. Do you offer commercial/contractor accounts?

Yes, we have special programs for landscape professionals, property managers, and commercial accounts. Benefits include discounted pricing, dedicated account representatives, job-site delivery, and flexible payment terms. Contractors can apply online or contact our commercial sales team at commercial@aquatwig.com. Requirements include proof of business (license, insurance, etc.) and references. We also offer training programs to help contractors become certified installers of our systems, which comes with additional benefits and recognition.

21. How do I reset my password?

If you've forgotten your password, click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page of our website. You'll be prompted to enter the email address associated with your account, and we'll send you a link to create a new password. This link expires after 24 hours for security. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact customer support. For security reasons, our staff cannot see or change your password - we can only send reset links to the email on file.

22. Can I change or cancel my order?

We can often modify or cancel orders if contacted quickly. For the best chance of making changes, call us immediately at (212) 555-1234 during business hours. Orders that have already been packed or shipped cannot be modified. Custom or special-order items typically cannot be canceled once production has begun. If your order has shipped, you may need to wait to receive it and then initiate a return (return shipping at your expense unless the item is defective). Our customer service team can explain your specific options.

23. Do you offer gift cards?

Yes! AquaTwig gift cards are available in any amount from $25 to $500. They can be purchased online, in our showroom, or by phone. E-gift cards are delivered via email within minutes, while physical gift cards are mailed via first-class mail (allow 5-7 business days for delivery). Gift cards never expire and can be used toward any products or services we offer. They're perfect for gardeners, homeowners, or anyone interested in water-efficient irrigation solutions. Gift cards cannot be redeemed for cash except where required by law.

24. How do I request a catalog?

Our full-color product catalog is available in digital format on our website. For a printed copy, you can request one through the "Catalog Request" form online, by emailing catalog@aquatwig.com, or by calling (212) 555-1234. Please allow 2-3 weeks for delivery of printed catalogs. We also offer specialized catalogs for commercial clients and contractors - let us know if you'd like these versions. Catalogs are updated annually in January with our newest products and offerings.

25. What's your privacy policy?

We take your privacy seriously. Our comprehensive privacy policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it. You can review our full privacy policy on our website. We never sell your personal information to third parties. You can update your communication preferences or request deletion of your data by contacting privacy@aquatwig.com. By using our website or providing your information, you consent to our collection and use of your information as described in the privacy policy.

26. How do I unsubscribe from emails?

Every email we send includes an unsubscribe link at the bottom. Clicking this will immediately remove you from that specific mailing list. You can also manage all your communication preferences by logging into your account on our website or by contacting customer service at unsubscribe@aquatwig.com. Please allow up to 10 business days for your preferences to fully process across all our systems. Transactional emails (order confirmations, shipping notices, etc.) cannot be opted out of as they contain important information about your purchases.

27. Do you have an affiliate program?

Yes, our affiliate program allows bloggers, influencers, and website owners to earn commissions by recommending AquaTwig products. The program pays a percentage of qualifying sales generated through your unique affiliate links. You can apply through the "Affiliates" section of our website. Approved affiliates get access to marketing materials, product images, and a dashboard to track performance. Commissions are paid monthly via PayPal or direct deposit. We have strict guidelines about how our products can be represented - please review these before applying.

28. How do I apply for a job at AquaTwig?

We're always looking for talented individuals to join our team! Current openings are listed in the "Careers" section of our website. You can apply online by submitting your resume and completing our application form. For positions not currently listed, you may submit a general application to hr@aquatwig.com. We keep applications on file for six months and will contact you if a suitable position becomes available. Due to volume, we can only respond to candidates we wish to interview further.

29. Do you sponsor events or donate products?

AquaTwig supports community gardens, educational programs, and environmental initiatives through product donations and sponsorships. Requests should be submitted at least 60 days in advance using the form in our "Community" section. Due to high demand, we can only fulfill a small percentage of requests. Priority is given to organizations focused on water conservation, urban agriculture, and environmental education in communities we serve. Monetary donations are extremely limited and typically reserved for long-term partners.

30. How do I report a product issue or safety concern?

Customer safety is our top priority. To report a product issue or safety concern, please contact our quality assurance team immediately at safety@aquatwig.com or call our toll-free safety hotline at 1-800-555-6789 (available 24/7). Please provide as many details as possible, including product name/number, manufacturing date (if available), and a description of the issue. For defective products, photos or videos showing the problem are extremely helpful. We investigate all reports promptly and will contact you with any follow-up questions.

31. What languages do you support?

Our primary customer support is available in English and Spanish during business hours. Many of our product manuals are available in multiple languages (English, Spanish, French) on our website. We're working to expand our language support - if you need assistance in another language, let us know and we'll do our best to accommodate you. Our website has basic machine translation available for many languages, though some technical details may not translate perfectly.

32. How do I provide feedback about my experience?

We value your feedback! You can share your experience through our customer satisfaction survey (sent after purchases and support interactions), by emailing feedback@aquatwig.com, or by leaving a review on our Google Business profile or other review platforms. For more detailed feedback, our customer experience team would be happy to speak with you - just request a callback through our contact form. All feedback is reviewed by management and used to improve our products and services.

33. Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes, we offer virtual consultations via video call for customers who can't visit our showroom or prefer remote assistance. To schedule one, fill out our consultation request form and select "Virtual Consultation" as your preferred method. You'll receive a link to join the video call at your scheduled time. Before your consultation, we recommend measuring your space and taking photos or video to share during the call. Virtual consultations typically last 30-60 minutes and cover the same topics as in-person meetings.

34. How do I access my order history?

Registered users can view their complete order history by logging into their account on our website. From your account dashboard, click "Order History" to see past purchases, download invoices, and check the status of current orders. If you checked out as a guest, you can access your order history by entering your order number and email address in the "Guest Order Lookup" tool. For orders more than three years old, please contact customer service as they may not appear in your online history but can still be retrieved from our archives.

35. What if I receive a damaged item?

If your order arrives damaged, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of delivery at support@aquatwig.com or (212) 555-9012. Have your order number ready and provide photos of the damaged item and packaging. We'll arrange for a replacement to be sent at no cost to you. For large items, we may need to send a freight company to inspect the damage before processing your claim. In most cases, you won't need to return the damaged item - we may ask you to recycle or dispose of it locally.

36. How do I update my account information?

You can update your personal information, password, and communication preferences by logging into your account on our website. Click "Account Settings" to make changes. For security reasons, some information changes (like email address) may require verification. If you need help updating your information or can't access your account, contact our customer service team. Business account holders should contact their account representative for changes to company information or authorized users on the account.

37. Do you offer recycling for old irrigation equipment?

We're committed to sustainable practices and offer recycling options for old irrigation components. In our New York showroom, we accept plastic tubing, drip emitters, and controllers for recycling (some fees may apply for large quantities). For other locations, check with your local waste management provider about recycling plastic and metal irrigation parts. We also offer trade-in discounts when you upgrade to newer, more water-efficient AquaTwig systems - ask our sales team for details on current promotions.

38. How do I request a press kit or media information?

Members of the media can request our press kit by emailing press@aquatwig.com with your name, media outlet, and intended use of the information. Our press kit includes company background, high-resolution images, executive bios, and product information sheets. We're happy to arrange interviews with our experts or provide additional materials for specific stories. For urgent media requests, please call our media line at (212) 555-2468 and clearly state you're a journalist on deadline.

39. How do I suggest a new product or feature?

We welcome suggestions for new products or improvements to existing ones! You can submit your ideas through the "Product Feedback" form on our website. While we can't promise to implement every suggestion, all submissions are reviewed by our product development team. Please understand that by submitting an idea, you agree it becomes our property to use or not use as we see fit, without compensation. We cannot accept unsolicited product samples or designs for legal reasons.

40. How can I contact the executive team?

For matters requiring executive attention, you can write to: AquaTwig Executive Office, 100 Bleecker St, New York, NY 10013. You may also email executives@aquatwig.com, though please note this inbox is monitored by staff who triage messages to the appropriate executive. For urgent concerns that haven't been resolved through normal customer service channels, you can request executive escalation by calling (212) 555-1234 and asking for the Executive Customer Resolution team during business hours.
